nutritional consulting

So, you've just been diagnosed with a health condition...

...and you're totally overwhelmed. All the things you could eat before are off-limits. What do you do? You call us. Get a chef's perspective on how to navigate the grocery store, the kitchen, the farmer's market. We talk, we look through your kitchen. We shop for food. I leave you empowered, with a fridge full of food that's safe for you to eat. And you'll even get a week of unlimited e-mail support after your consultation. You can even combine your consultation with cooking lessons, so that you feel ready to take on your new lifestyle head-on where it counts: the kitchen.

special occasions and special requests

We are able to take on a very limited number of special requests, but if you need an autoimmune-friendly holiday meal, paleo truffles for a significant other's birthday, or perhaps an allergy-free engagement dinner for just you and your honey... we might just be able to help you out. Don't hesitate; no request goes unconsidered!
